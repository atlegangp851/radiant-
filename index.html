Couples Counseling

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of counselling services do you offer?

We offer Personal Counselling, Teenage Counselling and Couple Counselling

How can counselling help me?

Counselling can help you develop a greater understanding of yourself, your emotions, and your experiences. It can equip you with tools to manage difficult emotions, improve communication skills, build healthier relationships, and achieve your goals.

What is online therapy?

Online therapy allows you to receive mental health services remotely through a secure video conferencing platform. Sessions are confidential and offer a flexible alternative to traditional in-person therapy..

How do I book an online therapy session?

Scheduling your online therapy session with us is easy and convenient! We use Calendly, an online scheduling tool that allows you to view our available appointment slots and book directly at your convenience.

How often should I attend therapy sessions?

The frequency of sessions will depend on your individual needs and goals. We will discuss this with you during your initial consultation and adjust it as needed throughout your therapy journey
